Under the Blood Moon

The Blood Ravens take their name from the crimson moon that hangs low over the battlefield — a symbol of vigilance, resilience, and the bond between ravens that soar above the fray. We are a competitive guild forged in the crucible of the mythic tiers, where every player pushes to sharpen their craft and elevate those around them.

Our squads — Prime, Nemesis, Revnant, and BR2 — are more than teams; we are a brotherhood of ravens, each bringing our own strength to the flock. Whether you're a seasoned immortal or a rising star, the Blood Ravens welcome those who dare to fly higher.
